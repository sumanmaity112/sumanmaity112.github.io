&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There was a big building. Suddenly it took over by fire. Everyone was leaving the building. But one baby got stuck inside. One man went inside to rescue the baby but he got stuck there. He was trying to escape from the building but was unable to find the way. So, he sat  in a corner. There he saw something written on the wall in some different language. He saw one picture beside the text. The picture was about how to get out from the building in case of fire. He follows the instruction to get out from the building.
